Before you start

How to answer the questions

  1. Read the full question Each question is about how volcanoes form, erupt, or behave. Read it carefully before choosing.
  2. Pick one answer Click the option you think is correct. You can only select one answer per question.
  3. Check the feedback After you answer, a short explanation appears. Correct answers turn teal; wrong ones turn red.
  4. Press Next question The Next button unlocks after you answer. Use it to move through all 10 questions.
  5. Finish and review your score After question 10, click See results to view your final score out of 10.
  6. Play again for a new set Every round pulls 10 random questions from the full bank, so each attempt is different.
